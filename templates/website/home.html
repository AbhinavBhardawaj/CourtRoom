About CourtRoom

CourtRoom is a tool for instant access to Indian court case metadata and official orders. Designed for legal professionals, researchers, and the public to find details and recent judgments from select courts.

  • Fetches official data from Indian court portals
  • Easy-to-use modern dashboard built with Django & TailwindCSS
  • Open source (GitHub)
