Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. CourtRoom does not require or collect any sensitive personal information from users. The only information stored is what is necessary to provide and improve the case lookup service.

What We Log

  • User queries: When you search for a case, the type, number, and year are anonymously logged to monitor usage and improve performance.
  • Technical information: Non-identifying data such as browser type and access times may be automatically collected for security and analytics.

No Personal Data Collected

We do not require sign-up with real names, emails, or identifying details. No personal information is sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties, except as required by law.

Cookies

This website may use basic cookies or session storage for authentication (if user accounts are enabled) and user experience. We do not use cookies for advertising, profiling, or third-party marketing.

Third-Party Links

CourtRoom may link to external Indian court websites for official data. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Security

All data is stored securely and is accessible only to authorized maintainers for debugging and technical improvements.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us.
